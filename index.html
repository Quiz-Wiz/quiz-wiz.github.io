About Quiz-Wiz:

Quiz-wiz is an interactive Web application that uses AI technology to develop quiz questions for you based on a given text.

Who are we?

We are a group of university students looking to make the studying process easier for all who want to learn. As we all know, studying can sometimes leave us questioning what the most efficient method would be to build on our current knowledge. By creating Quiz-Wiz, we hope to offer an easy solution for studying.
